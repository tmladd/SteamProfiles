About

This website was created to make creating good-looking steam profiles much easier. Currently, it is very time-consuming and rage-inducing to create your own profile because of the way Steam handles formatting. Personally, I would regularly want to change things with my profile and make it look interesting, but I never found a place where I could easily do it so I ended up having to make them myself which took hours, all just for a profile. It didn't feel like it was worth the effort, and so I wanted to create a place where people can easily just click a button and have everything pre-formatted for them, taking out the headache of doing it. Many people love to tinker with their profiles and make them look unique and stand out, and this website makes it that much easier and accessible.

Personal Information

Hi, I am Tristan, and this is a website I made as a side project! I have created this website as something to help me learn and get better with programming, as something that will also help people out with something many people find to be fun. I've personally always hoped a website would pop up that would make messing around with Steam, the largest games provider for PC, to be much more accessible and allow it to be a more personalized and unique experience. I decided to take this upon myself to create.
